Everything you need to build a great ear

Four complementary modes that work together at your own pace.

Practice

Put your ears to the test. Each session plays a melodic interval and asks you to identify it from multiple choices. Configure your tonic key, choose interval groups, and set the number of questions. A detailed score breakdown shows exactly what you're nailing and what needs work.

Explore

Browse all 13 intervals at once in an interactive grid. Tap any interval to hear it played instantly in your chosen key. Perfect for building the mental "sound library" you'll draw on when recognizing intervals in real music.

Listen

Step through a curated sequence of intervals at your own pace. Enable spoken announcements so each interval name is read aloud before it plays — a powerful technique for anchoring sounds to their names. Great as a warm-up, passive session, or reference tool.

History

Practice and Listen session results are saved automatically so you can track your progress over time and see how your accuracy improves with each session.
