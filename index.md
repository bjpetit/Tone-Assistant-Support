Welcome to the official support page for **Tone Assistant**.

## Getting started

1. Install the latest version of Tone Assistant from the App Store.
2. Open the app and allow microphone access when prompted.
3. Follow the onboarding steps to configure your tone preferences.

## Troubleshooting

### The app does not hear my voice

- Confirm microphone permission is enabled in iOS Settings.
- Restart the app after changing permissions.
- Test recording with another app to confirm your microphone works.

### Suggestions feel off-tone

- Re-run onboarding and pick the tone profile closest to your use case.
- Provide a longer sample message so the app has more context.

## FAQ

**Do I need an account?**  
No. Tone Assistant can be used without account creation.

**Does Tone Assistant require an internet connection?**  
Some features may need connectivity for model updates and service access.
